Who is he?

He's sweet

Kirby is a good guy. He’ll be nice to you if you are nice in return. He is also extremely cute. Kirby lives in Dreamland, along with many Waddle-dee's (like that plushy little orange guy over there!) Most of the time, waddle-dees are friends, but sometimes they turn on us and work for King Dedede, who's kirby's main rival.

He's strong

Kirby may be pink and puffy, but he’s tough! You don’t want to be on his bad side. He swallows his enemies whole and uses their abilities to his advantage. There are lots of abilities Kirby can use, but my favorite is the sword.

Some of Kirby's Common Abilities
Image Name Description
Sword Slash your foes with a big sharp blade.
Cutter Throw sharp boomerang-like knives through enemies.
Ice Turn your rivals into ice cubes.
Hammer Whack 'em all over the head and conquer the world.
Jet Fly very fast and burn your foes with the heat from your super awesome jetpack.
Fire Burn.. BUUURRRNNNN!!
Wing Flap your wings and fly! Also shoot deadly feathers. What more could you want?

He has more abilities, but these are a few of the main ones.

He's sleepy

Like all of us, Kirby just wants some delicious food followed by a nice nap every now and then! He loves to doze off and relax whenever he can. Do we blame him? I think we will all gladly take any precious nap-time we can get!

Why is he my


FAVORITE?

Nostalgic

Kirby is over 30 years old! He’s been a part of many people’s childhoods, including my own! He will never NOT be cool.

Adventurous

Both myself and Kirby enjoy going on adventures and trying new things. That's what life's all about!

Cute & Brave

I love Kirby because he’s proof that you can be cute and tough at the same time. Look at that pink puffy face! He can kill us all!
